PARK HYATT AVIARA RESORT — CONCIERGE KNOWLEDGE BASE
====================================================

PROPERTY OVERVIEW
-----------------
Park Hyatt Aviara Resort, Golf Club & Spa
7100 Aviara Resort Drive, Carlsbad, CA 92011
Phone: (760) 448-1234
Forbes Five-Star rated luxury resort on the Southern California coast.
200 guestrooms and suites, many with ocean or lagoon views.

CHECK-IN / CHECK-OUT
--------------------
Check-in: 4:00 PM (early check-in available upon request, subject to availability)
Check-out: 12:00 PM (late checkout may be arranged — contact concierge by 10:00 AM)
Express checkout available via in-room TV or front desk.

DINING
------
Argyle Steakhouse: Signature fine dining. Dinner nightly 5:30–9:30 PM. Reservations recommended. Smart casual dress code (no shorts, athletic wear, or sandals). Private dining room available for groups of 8–14.

Vivace: Mediterranean-inspired. Breakfast 6:30–11:00 AM, Lunch 11:30 AM–2:00 PM, Dinner 5:30–9:00 PM. Outdoor terrace with golf course views. Family-friendly.

Ember & Rye: Casual bar and lounge. Open daily 11:00 AM–11:00 PM. Craft cocktails, small plates, live music on Friday/Saturday evenings.

California Bistro: Poolside dining. Open seasonally 11:00 AM–5:00 PM. Burgers, salads, smoothies.

In-Room Dining: Available 24 hours. Full menu until 10:00 PM, limited overnight menu after 10:00 PM.

Dietary Accommodations: All restaurants accommodate gluten-free, vegetarian, vegan, and common food allergies. Advance notice for severe allergies appreciated.

SPA & WELLNESS
--------------
Aviara Spa: Full-service spa with 20 treatment rooms.
Hours: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily.
Signature treatments: Aviara Signature Massage (80 min), Ocean Renewal Facial (60 min), Couples Suite experience.
Booking: Advance reservations strongly recommended, especially weekends.
Cancellation: 24-hour cancellation policy; late cancellations or no-shows charged at full rate.
Spa amenities: eucalyptus steam room, sauna, whirlpool, relaxation lounge, fitness center.

Fitness Center: Open 24 hours. Complimentary for hotel guests. Peloton bikes, free weights, cardio machines, yoga mats. Personal training available by appointment.

GOLF
----
Aviara Golf Club: 18-hole Arnold Palmer signature course.
Tee times: 7:00 AM – 3:00 PM (varies by season).
Booking: Guests may book up to 90 days in advance. Non-guests up to 14 days.
Rates: Vary by season. Includes cart. Club rentals available.
Pro Shop: Open 6:30 AM – 5:00 PM. Equipment, apparel, accessories.
Golf lessons: Private and group lessons available with PGA professionals.
Dress code: Collared shirts, golf slacks or shorts. No denim, no athletic shorts.

POOL & RECREATION
-----------------
Main Pool: Heated. Open 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM. Cabanas available for daily rental (book in advance — high demand on weekends).
Family Pool: Open 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM. Shallow entry, water toys.
Tennis Courts: Four lighted courts. Equipment rental available. Lesson packages available.
Basketball Court: Complimentary, open during daylight hours.
Hiking: Batiquitos Lagoon nature trail accessible from the resort (1.5 miles).

TRANSPORTATION
--------------
Airport: San Diego International Airport (SAN), approximately 30 minutes south.
Airport Transfers: Private car service available. Sedan: ~$120 one way. SUV: ~$160 one way. Book through concierge at least 24 hours in advance.
Valet Parking: $45/night for overnight guests. Self-parking available: $35/night.
Rental Cars: Enterprise on-site or nearby Hertz/Avis. Concierge can arrange.
Ride Share: Uber/Lyft pickup at main entrance.
